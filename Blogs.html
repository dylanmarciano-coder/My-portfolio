My challenges starting off in Computer Programming

Starting Computer Programming very difficult for me. As a STEM student during my Grade 11 and 12, I was expecting us to learn at least the basics of coding, or something adjacent to it. But to my dismay, we did not do such things. We primarily focused on mathematics and different sciences like geology and physics.

All these being said, I was very intimidated by coding, without any background or any sort of knowledge on how to do it, I had always viewed it as an enigma, something that I was very interested in, but also something I had zero undertanding of.

Our first meeting in the Computer Programming course on confirmed my fear, that I was going to be like fish out of water beside my classmates. All the different terminologies were vague to me, and all the information was making me doubt my capabilities to continue on with the Computer Science course as a whole. But I knew I had to keep pushing, to push past my fears and doubts and move forward. From there, I began learning more about C++ through our different assignments, and slowly refine my coding skills more and more with each week that passes until I finally became confident with my coding skills.
